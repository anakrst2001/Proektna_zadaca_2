Best book of all time?

It's hard to choose one, isn't it?

Given there are a lot of amazing writers it's hard to choose one favourite book. My personal favourites include:

  • Literally anything written by Dostoyevsky
  • The hunchback of Notre-Dame by Victor Hugo
  • Les misérables by Victor Hugo
  • The bell jar by Sylvia Plath
  • To kill a mockingbird by Harper Lee
  • The stranger by Albert Camus
  • The storyteller by Jodi Picoult
  • The little prince by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

Share your favourite book with the community and explain why you like it so much.
