Important life lessons from Mr. Miyagi

Mr. Miyagi's best quotes

Karate Kid has taught me many life lessons and therefore is my favourite movie. My personal favourites are:

  • It's okay to lose to opponent. It's never okay to lose to fear.
  • For a man with no forgiveness in his heart, life is a worse punishment than death.
  • A lie becomes the truth only if people want to believe it.
  • Never put passion in front of principle. Even if you win, you'll lose!
  • You should trust the quality of what you know, not the quantity.
  • First learn how to stand, then learn how to fly.
  • If it comes from inside of you, it's always the right one.
  • There's no such thing as a bad student, there's only a bad teacher.

Best book of all time?

It's hard to choose one, isn't it?

Given there are a lot of amazing writers it's hard to choose one favourite book. My personal favourites include:

  • Literally anything written by Dostoyevsky
  • The hunchback of Notre-Dame by Victor Hugo
  • Les misérables by Victor Hugo
  • The bell jar by Sylvia Plath
  • To kill a mockingbird by Harper Lee
  • The stranger by Albert Camus
  • The storyteller by Jodi Picoult
  • The little prince by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry
